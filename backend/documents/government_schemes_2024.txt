# Government Financial Schemes in India - 2024

## Retirement & Pension Schemes

### Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
**Eligibility:**
- Age: 18-40 years
- Must have a savings bank account
- Must have Aadhaar number

**Features:**
- Guaranteed monthly pension: ₹1,000 to ₹5,000
- Pension starts at age 60
- Government co-contributes 50% of total contribution (max ₹1,000/year for 5 years)
- Tax benefit available under Section 80CCD

**Monthly Contribution Examples (for ₹5,000 pension at 60):**
- Join at 18: ₹210/month
- Join at 25: ₹376/month
- Join at 30: ₹577/month
- Join at 35: ₹902/month
- Join at 40: ₹1,454/month

**Death Benefit:**
- Spouse receives same pension until death
- After spouse's death, nominee receives corpus amount

### Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana (PMVVY)
**Eligibility:**
- Age: 60 years and above
- Senior citizens only

**Features:**
- Investment limit: ₹15 lakh per senior citizen
- Guaranteed return: 7.4% per annum
- Pension frequency: Monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly
- Term: 10 years
- Operated by LIC of India

**Pension Amount:**
- ₹15 lakh investment gives ₹9,250/month pension
- Tax-free pension income
- Premature exit: Available after 3 years (with 2% penalty)

### National Pension System (NPS)
**Eligibility:**
- Age: 18-70 years
- Indian citizens (resident and non-resident)

**Account Types:**
- Tier 1: Mandatory retirement account (with lock-in)
- Tier 2: Voluntary savings account (no lock-in, no tax benefit)

**Fund Options:**
- Equity (E): Up to 75% allocation (50% for government employees)
- Corporate Bonds (C)
- Government Securities (G)
- Alternative Investment Fund (A): Up to 5%

**Returns (Historical):**
- Equity funds: 10-12% average
- Hybrid funds: 8-10% average
- Government bonds: 7-9% average

## Small Savings Schemes

### Public Provident Fund (PPF)
**Interest Rate:** 7.1% per annum (compounded annually)
**Maturity:** 15 years (extendable in blocks of 5 years)

**Investment Details:**
- Minimum: ₹500/year
- Maximum: ₹1,50,000/year
- Can invest lump sum or up to 12 installments

**Loan Facility:**
- Available from 3rd to 6th year
- Maximum: 25% of balance at end of 2nd preceding year
- Interest: 1% above PPF rate

**Withdrawal Rules:**
- Partial withdrawal from 7th year
- Maximum: 50% of balance at end of 4th preceding year
- Premature closure: After 5 years (with 1% interest penalty)

### Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)
**Eligibility:**
- Age: 60 years and above
- Age 55-60: Retired defense personnel/retired under VRS

**Interest Rate:** 8.2% per annum (quarterly payout)

**Investment Details:**
- Minimum: ₹1,000
- Maximum: ₹30 lakh (increased in Budget 2023)
- Maturity: 5 years (extendable by 3 years)

**Tax Treatment:**
- Investment: Deduction under 80C
- Interest: Taxable (TDS if interest > ₹50,000)

### Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
**Eligibility:**
- Girl child below 10 years
- Maximum 2 accounts per family (3 for twins/triplets)

**Interest Rate:** 8.2% per annum (highest among small savings)

**Investment Details:**
- Minimum: ₹250/year
- Maximum: ₹1,50,000/year
- Contribution period: 15 years from account opening

**Withdrawal Rules:**
- 50% withdrawal for education/marriage after girl turns 18
- Premature closure: For marriage after 18
- Account matures: 21 years from opening or marriage

### Post Office Monthly Income Scheme (POMIS)
**Interest Rate:** 7.4% per annum (monthly payout)

**Investment Details:**
- Single account: Maximum ₹9 lakh
- Joint account: Maximum ₹15 lakh
- Maturity: 5 years

**Payout:**
- Interest paid monthly on 1st of next month
- ₹9 lakh investment = ₹5,550/month income

### National Savings Certificate (NSC)
**Interest Rate:** 7.7% per annum (compounded annually)
**Maturity:** 5 years

**Features:**
- Minimum: ₹1,000
- No maximum limit
- Interest reinvested automatically
- Accrued interest qualifies for 80C deduction

### Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP)
**Interest Rate:** 7.5% per annum
**Doubling Period:** 115 months (9 years 7 months)

**Features:**
- Minimum: ₹1,000
- No maximum limit
- Premature encashment after 2.5 years

## Women-Specific Schemes

### Mahila Samman Savings Certificate (MSSC)
**Launched:** Budget 2023
**Available:** April 2023 to March 2025

**Features:**
- Interest Rate: 7.5% per annum
- Maturity: 2 years
- Maximum: ₹2 lakh per person
- Eligibility: Women or girl child
- Partial withdrawal: 40% after 1 year

### PM Jan Dhan Yojana
**Features:**
- Zero balance account
- Free RuPay debit card
- Accidental insurance: ₹2 lakh
- Overdraft facility: Up to ₹10,000

## Housing Schemes

### Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)
**Eligibility (Income Categories):**
- EWS: Annual income up to ₹3 lakh
- LIG: Annual income ₹3-6 lakh
- MIG-I: Annual income ₹6-12 lakh
- MIG-II: Annual income ₹12-18 lakh

**Interest Subsidy (Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme):**
- EWS/LIG: 6.5% subsidy on ₹6 lakh loan (20 years)
- MIG-I: 4% subsidy on ₹9 lakh loan (20 years)
- MIG-II: 3% subsidy on ₹12 lakh loan (20 years)

**Net Present Value of Subsidy:**
- EWS/LIG: Up to ₹2.67 lakh
- MIG-I: Up to ₹2.35 lakh
- MIG-II: Up to ₹2.30 lakh

## Insurance Schemes

### Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)
**Coverage:** ₹2 lakh life insurance
**Premium:** ₹436/year (auto-debited)
**Eligibility:** Age 18-55 years with bank account

### Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
**Coverage:**
- Death: ₹2 lakh
- Total permanent disability: ₹2 lakh
- Partial permanent disability: ₹1 lakh

**Premium:** ₹20/year
**Eligibility:** Age 18-70 years with bank account

### Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)
**Coverage:** ₹5 lakh per family per year
**Eligibility:** Deprived rural families and identified urban workers
**Covers:** Secondary and tertiary hospitalization

## Agricultural Schemes

### PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
**Benefit:** ₹6,000/year in three installments
**Eligibility:** All landholding farmer families
**Exclusions:** Income tax payers, government employees

### Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
**Features:**
- Interest rate: 7% (additional 3% subvention available)
- Credit limit: Based on landholding and cultivation
- Includes crop insurance and personal accident insurance

## Education & Skill Schemes

### PM Vidya Lakshmi Portal
**Features:**
- Single window for education loans
- 126 participating banks
- Loans from ₹4 lakh to ₹7.5 lakh without collateral

### Skill India (PMKVY)
**Features:**
- Free skill training
- Financial reward on certification
- Placement assistance
- Recognition of prior learning

## Comparison: Best Schemes by Objective

### For Retirement Planning:
1. NPS (market-linked, tax benefits)
2. PPF (guaranteed returns, tax-free)
3. APY (guaranteed pension)

### For Regular Income:
1. SCSS (8.2%, seniors)
2. PMVVY (7.4%, seniors)
3. POMIS (7.4%, general)

### For Short-Term Goals:
1. NSC (5 years, 7.7%)
2. MSSC (2 years, 7.5%)
3. Bank FD (flexible tenure)

### For Girl Child:
1. SSY (highest return 8.2%)
2. PPF (for general savings)

### For Tax Saving:
1. ELSS (3 year lock-in, market returns)
2. PPF (15 years, guaranteed)
3. NPS (additional 80CCD(1B))
